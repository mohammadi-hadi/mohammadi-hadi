## PhD Thesis

**"From Tokens to Thoughts: Explainable NLP for Understanding Large Language Models"**
*Utrecht University, 2025*

My doctoral research develops frameworks for understanding and explaining decisions made by large language models, with focus on:
- Cultural and moral reasoning in LLMs
- Robust NLP systems against adversarial inputs
- Annotation quality and reliability frameworks
- Explainable AI methodologies
